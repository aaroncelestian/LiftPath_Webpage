Your data stays on your device. LiftPath does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information, workout data, or health metrics on external servers. You have complete control over your data.

  • All data stored locally using SwiftData
  • No external servers or cloud storage
  • No tracking or analytics
  • You control all data exports and sharing

Features

  • Strength Training: Log exercises, sets, reps, weight, and RPE
  • Rest Timer: Automatic rest timer between sets
  • Personal Records: Track your PRs automatically
  • Body Measurements: Record circumference measurements with trend charts
  • Activity Tracking: Log cardio and sports activities with calorie estimation
  • Apple Health: Read weight, body fat, nutrition, sleep, and HRV data
  • Progress Charts: Visualize your training volume and body metrics
  • Template Import: Import workout programs from text files
  • Data Export: Share data with coaches via JSON or PDF
  • Dark Mode: Full support for iOS dark mode

System Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone or iPad
  • Optional: Apple Health for enhanced features
