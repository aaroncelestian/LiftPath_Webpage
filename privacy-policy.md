# Privacy Policy for LiftPath

**Last Updated: December 21, 2025**

## Overview

LiftPath is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

## Data Collection and Storage

**We Do Not Collect Any Personal Data**

LiftPath does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information, workout data, health metrics, or any other data on external servers. All data you enter into LiftPath is stored locally on your device only.

## Local Data Storage

- All workout sessions, exercise logs, and training data are stored locally on your iOS device using SwiftData
- Body measurements, personal records, and activity logs remain on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it

## Apple Health Integration

LiftPath may request permission to read data from Apple Health, including:
- Body weight and body fat percentage
- Nutrition data (calories, macros)
- Sleep data
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Active energy burned

**Important:**
- This data is only read from Apple Health and used within the app
- We do not write data to Apple Health
- We do not transmit this data to any external servers
- You control access through iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices

## User Control Over Data

You have complete control over your data:

- **Export**: You can manually export your workout data as JSON or PDF files through the app's export features
- **Sharing**: No data is shared without your explicit action
- **Deletion**: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device
- **Backup**: Your data may be included in iCloud backups if you have iCloud Backup enabled for the app

## Data Sharing

LiftPath does not share your data with any third parties. If you choose to export your data:

- You control who receives the exported files
- Exports are generated locally on your device
- You decide whether to share with coaches, AI assistants, or others
- No automatic sharing or transmission occurs

## Children's Privacy

LiftPath does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for users who are at least 13 years old.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have full access to your data at all times
- You can delete your data by deleting the app
- You can export your data at any time through the app's export features
- No data retention policies apply as we do not store your data

---

**Summary**: LiftPath is a privacy-first application. Your workout data, health metrics, and personal information stay on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. You are in complete control.
